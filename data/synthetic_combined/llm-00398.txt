[english] This function utilizes nested context managers to ensure that all operations created within it follow a predictable, standardized naming hierarchy.

[python] def _name_scope(self, name=None, default_name=None, values=None):
    """Helper function to standardize op scope."""
    with tf.compat.v1.name_scope(self.name):
      with tf.compat.v1.name_scope(
          name, default_name, values=values or []) as scope:
        yield scope

[english] By chaining `tf.compat.v1.name_scope` calls, we guarantee that the inner scope is uniquely identified within the parent scope.
