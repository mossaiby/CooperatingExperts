[english] How can I create a utility to output structured field definitions into Markdown format while automatically calculating necessary column widths?
[english] This function calculates the required display dimensions for field data. It retrieves the initial lengths from the fields, prints the header to determine its size, and then adjusts the target width to accommodate both the header and the content.

[python] def outputFieldMarkdown(self):
        """
        Sends the field definitions ot standard out
        """
        f, d = self.getFieldsColumnLengths()

        fc, dc = self.printFieldsHeader(f, d)
        f = max(fc, f)
        d = max(dc, d)
        self.printFields(f, d)

[english] The use of `max()` ensures that the final output columns (`f` and `d`) are wide enough to contain whatever is widest—the original field definitions or the generated header.
