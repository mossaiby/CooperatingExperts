[english] How do I gracefully shut down an event listener that uses multiple threads and queues?
[english] This method implements a graceful shutdown sequence by first signaling termination to the event processing queue, then waiting for dependent components to finish their cleanup.

[python] def deactivate(self):
        """When deactivated the :class:`PortEventListener` will not run
        anything.
        """
        self.event_queue.put(self.TERMINATE_SIGNAL)
        self.dispatcher.join()
        self.detector.terminate()
        self.detector.join()
