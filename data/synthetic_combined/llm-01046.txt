[english] To ensure every input has a comparable scale regardless of its original size, we calculate the standard deviation and use it as a divisor for all elements.

[python] def __normalize_onsets(onsets):
    '''Maps onset strength function into the range [0, 1]'''

    norm = onsets.std(ddof=1)
    if norm > 0:
        onsets = onsets / norm
    return onsets
