[english] While this correctly reverses position, remember that it only targets translation and does not affect any rotation or scaling already applied.
[english] What happens to the matrix if the object's current position is at (0, 0, 0)?
[english] If the coordinates are all zero, applying a negative translation of zero will result in calling `glTranslatef(0, 0, 0)`, which means no transformation occurs.
