[english] How can I dynamically create a comprehensive regular expression by reading multiple individual patterns from a configuration file?
[english] To fulfill this request, we read all lines from a specified filename and treat each line as an individual pattern. We then combine these individual patterns into one large regex using the OR operator ('|') before compiling it.
