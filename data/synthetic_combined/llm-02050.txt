[english] How do I dynamically drop a UNIQUE constraint defined on an hstore field in my database model?
[english] This approach first determines the exact naming convention used for the unique constraint associated with your specified hstore keys. It then constructs and executes raw SQL to remove that specific uniqueness requirement.
<switch:python>
[python] def _drop_hstore_unique(self, model, field, keys):
        """Drops a UNIQUE constraint for the specified hstore keys."""

        name = self._unique_constraint_name(
            model._meta.db_table, field, keys)
        sql = self.sql_hstore_unique_drop.format(name=self.quote_name(name))
        self.execute(sql)

[english] What happens if the constraint specified by the field and keys does not actually exist in the database?
