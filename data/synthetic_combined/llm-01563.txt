[english] How can I automatically register my custom parsing classes with a central registry using a decorator?
[english] This function acts as a decorator factory to automate the registration of parser components. It captures either an explicit name or derives one from the decorated object's metadata, then adds it to a global collection.

        The class is registered with its name unless directname is provided.

    class_dir_list = _directives
