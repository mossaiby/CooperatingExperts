[english] How do I define a learnable parameter in TensorFlow that is guaranteed to remain strictly positive during training?
[english] This function creates a constraint by first generating an unconstrained variable, applying a softplus activation and clamping it to a minimum value, then packaging this result into a deterministic distribution.
<switch:python>
[python] def trainable_positive_deterministic(shape, min_loc=1e-3, name=None):
  """Learnable Deterministic distribution over positive reals."""
  with tf.compat.v1.variable_scope(
      None, default_name="trainable_positive_deterministic"):
    unconstrained_loc = tf.compat.v1.get_variable("unconstrained_loc", shape)
    loc = tf.maximum(tf.nn.softplus(unconstrained_loc), min_loc)
    rv = ed.Deterministic(loc=loc, name=name)
