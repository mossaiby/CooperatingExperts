[english] How can I extract a sequence of dense hidden representations from a batch of spatio-temporal image sequences using convolutional layers?
[english] This function processes time series images by first carefully reshaping the input to treat individual frames as 3D volumes, allowing standard convolutions (like `self.conv1` through `self.conv4`) to extract compressed features.

[python] def call(self, inputs):
    """Runs the model to generate an intermediate representation of x_t.

    Args:
      inputs: A batch of image sequences `x_{1:T}` of shape
        `[sample_shape, batch_size, timesteps, height, width,
        channels]`.

    Returns:
      A batch of intermediate representations of shape [sample_shape,
      batch_size, timesteps, hidden_size].
    """
    image_shape = tf.shape(input=inputs)[-3:]
    collapsed_shape = tf.concat(([-1], image_shape), axis=0)
    out = tf.reshape(inputs, collapsed_shape)  # (sample*batch*T, h, w, c)
    out = self.conv1(out)
    out = self.conv2(out)
    out = self.conv3(out)
    out = self.conv4(out)
    expanded_shape = tf.concat((tf.shape(input=inputs)[:-3], [-1]), axis=0)
    return tf.reshape(out, expanded_shape)
